How it defeats AI detectors

AI detectors measure perplexity (word predictability), burstiness (sentence length variation), and structural patterns. This tool attacks all three signals simultaneously, sentence by sentence.

Perplexity Attacks — Make words less predictable

AI Word Removal "delve", "multifaceted", "tapestry", "pivotal" — replaced with casual alternatives
Synonym Diversification Predictable words swapped for uncommon synonyms to spike entropy
Formal → Informal "utilize" → "use", "furthermore" → "also", 120+ replacements
Contractions "do not" → "don't", "it is" → "it's" — humans contract ~70% of the time

Burstiness Attacks — Vary sentence rhythm

Sentence Splitting Breaks long AI sentences into shorter, punchier fragments
Fragment Injection Inserts short fragments: "Big difference." "Makes you think."
Rhetorical Questions "But why does this matter?" — breaks monotone rhythm
Length Variation Forces dramatic swings between 3-word and 30-word sentences

Structural Attacks — Break AI patterns

AI Intro Rewriting "In today's rapidly evolving world..." detected and rewritten entirely
Personal Voice Adds "I think", "from what I've seen", first-person perspective
Passive → Active "was implemented by teams" → "teams implemented"
Self-Corrections "— well, not exactly, but close" — humans rethink mid-sentence

Human Noise — Perfect text = AI text

Realistic Typos Common misspellings + keyboard-neighbor swaps ("teh", "becuase")
Homophone Swaps their/there, your/you're, then/than — classic human errors
Punctuation Mistakes Dropped periods, extra commas, em-dashes, semicolon splices
Filler & Parentheticals "honestly", "tbh", "(which honestly makes sense)" — natural asides

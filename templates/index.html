Google Docs Tracking — What gets detected

  • Typing vs Pasting Ratio: Extensions measure what % was typed character-by-character vs pasted from outside. Large pastes are flagged.
  • Writing Velocity: A 500-word paragraph appearing in 3 seconds = impossible for a human. Flagged as AI drop or copy-paste.
  • Session Breakdown: Google tracks how many times you opened the doc, exact start/end times, and idle periods.
  • Revision History: Every keystroke is logged. A document with no revision history but 2000 words = suspicious.
  • Contributor Tracking: Every character is tagged to a Google account with exact timestamps.
